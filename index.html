Core Mandate

Our Mission

To provide an engaging and professional military roleplay experience, promoting community pride and delivering high-quality gameplay for all members.

Our History

Established in mid-2025, our community has seen consistent growth. We are dedicated to building a respected and prosperous future for our members.

Structure

Our community is led by a dedicated command staff and organized into specialized divisions, ensuring operational excellence and a clear path for progression.

Recruitment Process

Step 1: Application

Your career begins by joining our official Roblox Group. This serves as your formal application to become a member of the community.

Step 2: Training

To be eligible for promotion, all recruits must successfully complete Basic Military Training (BMT) to learn our standards and procedures.

Step 3: Integration

Upon graduation from BMT, you may apply for a specialized division and must join our Discord server for all official communications.
